Responsibility

Built for responsible learning.

We don't promise the future of AI is risk-free. We do promise the tutor is honest about what it is.

  • Learning-first design. Won't write your homework. Will help you understand how to think about it.
  • Calibrated honesty. Every substantive reply carries a confidence signal — high, medium, low, "I'm not sure."
  • Bias awareness. Mercurius is upfront about the limits of its training data and the perspectives it might be missing.
  • Academic integrity. Asking for an essay redirects to the thinking behind it.
  • Privacy-conscious. No personal data required. Local chat history stays on your device.

What we don't claim

Honest about the limits.

Mercurius is an AI built on a large language model. That means it can still produce confident-sounding mistakes. The whole product is designed around teaching you to notice when that's happening — not pretending it doesn't.

We don't claim Mercurius replaces a teacher. We don't claim it guarantees better grades. We don't sell student data, and we don't require an account to learn. If any of those things change, this page changes too.
