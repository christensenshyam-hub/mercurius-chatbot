Features

Built to teach thinking,
not replace it.

Most AI products give you answers. Mercurius gives you the muscle to evaluate them.

Socratic learning

Guided questions, not instant answers. Every conversation pushes you one step further than you came in.

Critical thinking

Ask, evaluate, understand. Mercurius flags confidence, surfaces tradeoffs, and never presents contested claims as settled.

AI literacy

Learn how AI actually works, when to use it, and when not. The same concepts experts argue about — in language built for students.

Why it matters

A different bar for "useful."

The fastest answer is rarely the most useful one. When students copy an answer they don't understand, they get short-term relief and long-term debt — the next time the topic comes up, they'll be exactly as stuck as they were before.

Mercurius is built so the time you spend with it pays off twice: once when you finish the conversation, and again every time you hit a related question afterward. Guided, not given.
